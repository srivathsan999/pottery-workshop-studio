Our Story

A journey of passion, craftsmanship, and the timeless art of pottery

How It Began

Earth & Clay Studio was born from a simple belief: that working with clay is one of humanity's most fundamental and meditative practices. Our founder, after years of teaching pottery in various settings, dreamed of creating a space where this ancient art could be accessible to everyone.

In 2018, we opened our doors in a converted warehouse, filling it with natural light, quality wheels, and a welcoming atmosphere. What started as a small studio has grown into a community of makers, artists, and pottery enthusiasts.

Today, we're proud to offer workshops for all skill levels, from complete beginners to advanced ceramic artists looking to refine their craft.

Our Philosophy

Accessibility

We believe pottery should be accessible to everyone, regardless of experience or background. Our welcoming environment and patient instruction make it easy to begin your journey.

Mindfulness

Working with clay is a meditative practice. We encourage our students to slow down, be present, and find calm in the creative process.

Community

Our studio is more than a place to learn—it's a community of makers who support and inspire each other. We celebrate every creation, from first attempts to masterpieces.
